HOW IT WORKS

Freezer visualizes whatever audio is playing on your system. To capture your audio, your browser will ask you to share a screen or tab — this is how we access the audio stream. The video is immediately discarded; nothing is recorded or transmitted.

🛈 In the share dialog, pick any screen or tab that has audio playing. If you share a tab, only that tab's audio is captured. Sharing a screen captures all system audio.
Drag & drop .avs or .json preset files onto the visualizer F for fullscreen
